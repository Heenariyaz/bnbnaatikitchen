Our Story

Authentic Karnataka Flavours

Welcome to BNB Naati Kitchen — where every dish carries the soul of North Karnataka. Our recipes travel all the way from the kitchens of Bijapur, bringing you the true taste of Nati cooking to the heart of Bengaluru.

We use country chicken slow-cooked in traditional masalas, no shortcuts, no compromise. Our Mudde, Jolada Rotti, and Tahari are made just the way grandmothers made them — with patience, spice, and love.

Delivered fresh to your doorstep. Real food. Real taste.
